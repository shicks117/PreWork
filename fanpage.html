The Facts

Formed in 1983 in Los Angeles, the Red Hot Chili Peppers are an American Rock Band.

Consiting of vocalist Anthony Kiedis, bassist Flea, drummer Chad Smith, and guitarist John Frusciante, the Red Hot Chili Peppers are one of the best selling bands of all time at over 80 million records. They are considered the most successful band in the history of alternative rock thanks to the most number one singles (13), most cumulative weeks at number one (85), and most Billboard Top Ten songs (25).
